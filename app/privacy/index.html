JOES App – Privacy Policy

JOES does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app only displays publicly available Bitcoin blockchain information and price data obtained from third-party public APIs. No user information, identifiers, or usage data are collected or stored.

JOES does not use analytics, tracking technologies, advertising frameworks, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data.
